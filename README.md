A lightning-fast, offline desktop application designed for professionals and power users who work extensively with plain text files. **HighlighterTXT** bridges the gap between simple text editors and heavy word processors, adding powerful annotation and military-grade encryption features.

---

### 🚀 Core Features

#### 🟢 FREE Version
Perfect for daily text management, studying, and log analysis.
* **Instant Annotations:** Highlight text (Red, Yellow, Green, or Custom colors) with a single click.
* **Emoji Tags & Notes:** Attach visual markers (👍, 👎, ⭐, ❗) and text notes directly to specific lines.
* **Smart Summary Panel:** A dedicated side dock that lists all your highlights and notes for quick navigation.
* **Print-Ready PDF Export:** Export your annotated `.txt` files directly into clean, formatted PDFs ready for sharing or printing.

#### 🛡️ PRO Version (Advanced Security)
Designed for sensitive data, confidential logs, and private research.
* **`.hxt` Encryption:** Convert any text file into a password-protected, AES-256-GCM encrypted `.hxt` file.
* **Workspace Lock:** Instantly lock the app screen with a master password (Ctrl+Alt+L) to protect your active work from prying eyes.
* **Auto-Lock:** Configurable idle timeout that automatically locks your encrypted files when you step away.
* **Advanced Exports:** Export decrypted `.txt` copies or generate a full "Highlights Summary" document.
